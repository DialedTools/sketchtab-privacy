SketchTab Privacy Policy

Effective date: May 23, 2026

SketchTab - Draw on Any Page is a Chrome extension that lets users annotate webpages and PDFs by drawing, highlighting, adding shapes, adding text notes, erasing annotations, and saving the visible page with those annotations.

Data collection

SketchTab does not collect, sell, share, or transmit personal data through the extension.

Support form

If you submit the support form on this website, the information you enter, such as your name, email address, request type, device info, and message, is sent to the SketchTab support email so we can respond to your request. Do not submit sensitive information through the support form.

How the extension works

SketchTab runs locally in your browser. The extension uses Chrome permissions only to provide its annotation features on pages selected by the user.

Local storage

SketchTab may store basic extension preferences locally in your browser, such as selected tool, color, stroke size, and annotation settings. This information stays on your device and is not sent to external servers by the extension.

Website content

SketchTab can display an annotation overlay on webpages and PDFs that the user chooses to annotate. The extension does not collect or transmit the content of those pages.

Remote code

SketchTab does not use remote code. All JavaScript, CSS, HTML, and image assets are included inside the extension package.

Third parties

SketchTab does not sell user data. The support form uses a form handling service to deliver support requests to the support email address.

Contact

For privacy questions, use the support form or the contact email listed on the Chrome Web Store listing for SketchTab.
